about

At Sensai, we believe that the mental well-being of a person is just as important as their physical health. Our platform is designed to help individuals track their emotions, recognize patterns, and gain insights into their mental health.
By combining AI-driven analytics with personal journaling, Sensai empowers users to take charge of their emotional well-being, making informed decisions to improve their daily lives.
